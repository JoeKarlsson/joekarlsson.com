# Speaking

---

<p>Joe Karlsson (He/They) is a Data Engineer turned Developer Advocate. He is a [TEDx speaker](https://www.ted.com/talks/joe_karlsson_the_art_of_computer_science) and he has spoken at hundreds of technical conferences all over the globe. Joe specializes in making complex programming concepts approachable for developers of all levels.

Joe empowers developers to think creatively when building applications with a massive amount of data, through [demos](/work), [blogs](/blog), [videos](/work), or whatever else developers need.

- Software engineering
- Fun projects I'm working on
