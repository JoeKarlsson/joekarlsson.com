This page contains the Privacy Policy for JoeKarlsson.com.

### Analytics

This site uses a self-hosted instance of [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io/), a privacy-friendly analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect personal data. All data is aggregated and no individual visitors can be identified.

**Data collected:** Page URLs, referrer URLs, browser type, operating system, device type, and country. No IP addresses are stored.

**Custom events tracked:**

- **Outbound link clicks** - which external links visitors click
- **File downloads** - when visitors download files
- **404 pages** - which missing pages visitors encounter (helps me fix broken links)
- **Scroll depth** - how far visitors scroll on blog posts (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
- **Reading complete** - when a visitor finishes reading a blog post
- **Terminal commands** - which commands visitors use in the interactive homepage terminal
- **Contact social clicks** - which social platform links visitors click on the contact page
- **Newsletter CTA clicks** - clicks on the Substack subscribe button
- **Related post clicks** - clicks on related post suggestions

No personal information is collected through any of these events. All tracking is anonymous and aggregated.

### Cookies

This site does not set any cookies.

### Third-Party Services

- **Google Fonts** - Web fonts are loaded from Google's servers. Google may log requests per their [privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **External links** - This site links to third-party websites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

### Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach me through any of the platforms listed on my [contact page](/contact/).
