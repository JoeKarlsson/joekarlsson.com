## About Joe Karlsson

---

---

Joe Karlsson (He/They) is a Data Engineer turned Developer Advocate. He empowers developers to think creatively when building applications with a massive amount of data, through [demos](/work), [blogs](/blog), [videos](/work), or whatever else developers need.

Joe's career has taken him from building out database best practices and demos for MongoDB, architecting and building one of the largest eCommerce websites in North America at Best Buy, and teaching at one of the most highly-rated software development boot camps on Earth. Joe is also a TEDx Speaker, film buff, and avid [TikToker](https://twitter.com/JoeKarlsson1) and [Tweeter](https://twitter.com/JoeKarlsson1).
